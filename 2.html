For the questionnaire method, first of all, we should determine the direction and target of our survey.
Second, we should design our own company's questionnaire and design questions.
Third, the designed questionnaire should be sent to the respondents in the form of paper or email.
Fourth, the questionnaire should be collected and screened to retain useful information.
Finally, the data analysis meeting will be held to analyze the useful information and formulate the company's future plan to ensure the company's development.
